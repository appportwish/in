About Portwish

Your curated personal shopping & international sourcing platform.

Portwish was created to make overseas shopping feel easier, more organized, and more accessible without the hassle of manual ordering through chats and scattered catalogs.

We source and curate products from multiple countries including USA, Japan, Korea, Europe, and Indonesia bringing together everyday finds, lifestyle products, beauty essentials, wellness products, snacks, fashion, and unique items loved by customers worldwide. At Portwish, we focus on products that are useful, interesting, trending, and worth discovering.

"Our goal is to create a seamless shopping experience where customers can browse products, select available shipping batches, complete checkout easily, and track orders directly through their account."

More than just a typical personal shopping service, Portwish is built with a long-term vision to grow into a trusted global sourcing and curated product platform. We believe international shopping should feel exciting, transparent, and simple all in one place.
