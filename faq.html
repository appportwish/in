Portwish is a curated personal shopping & international sourcing platform that helps customers shop products from multiple countries including USA, Japan, Korea, Europe, and Indonesia.
We focus on curated everyday products such as beauty & skincare, body care, snacks & lifestyle products, vitamins & wellness, fashion & accessories, and home essentials.
Each country has its own shipping batch schedule. During checkout, customers will be able to choose their preferred available batch before completing payment.
Products from different countries are processed separately based on their respective shipping batches and sourcing locations.
  • Browse products on the website
  • Add items to cart
  • Select your preferred batch
  • Proceed to checkout
  • Complete payment
  • Track your order status directly from your account
